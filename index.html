What is Sensis?

Sensis comes from “sense” and the Greek “noēsis.” It’s the path where perception cultivates cognition.

What can Sensis do?

  • Define goals and constraints.
  • Search and judge sources efficiently.
  • Structure plans with milestones.
  • Produce clean, defensible outputs.

How does Sensis work?

  • Local: organize cognition and tasks.
  • Online: collaborate with AI.
  • Community: reuse path templates.
